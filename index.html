Welcome!

Characlatar is a simple and scientific calculator with character voices. You can choose between a cute girl or a cool boy, and they will speak in Japanese as you type. It's an educational, fun, and unique way to do math!

Switch character templates easily and enjoy cheerful voice feedback for every button press.
